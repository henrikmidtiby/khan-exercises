Are the electric forces between the two point charges attractive or repulsive?

At (X1, Y1) is a particle with charge Charge1 and at (X2, Y2) is a particle with charge Charge2.

Opposite charges attrack each others while similar charges repel each other.

The first charge is negative.

The charges are opposite and thus the electric force is attractive.

The charges are similar and thus the electric force is repulsive.
